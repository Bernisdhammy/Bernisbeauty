About Bernisbeauty

Our story, mission, and values

Our Story

Bernisbeauty was born from a simple belief: that skincare should be pure, effective, and kind to both our bodies and the planet.

Founded in 2015 by skincare enthusiasts Damilola Habeeb,Bernisbeauty began as a small batch operation in a factory in Accra,Ghana. Frustrated by the lack of truly natural products that actually worked, they set out to create formulations using only the finest ingredients nature has to offer.

What started as a passion project quickly grew into a beloved brand as word spread about our effective, luxurious products that deliver real results without compromising on ethics or sustainability.

Today, Bernisbeauty continues to grow while staying true to our founding principles. Every product we create is still made with the same care, attention to detail, and commitment to quality that defined our very first batch.

Our Mission & Values

Purity

We believe in the power of pure, natural ingredients. Our products contain only what your skin needs and nothing it doesn't.

Transparency

We're committed to full transparency about our ingredients, sourcing practices, and manufacturing processes.

Sustainability

Every decision we make considers its impact on the planet. From ingredients to packaging, we strive to minimize our environmental footprint.

Compassion

We believe in kindness toward all living beings. Our products are cruelty-free and we support initiatives that promote animal welfare.
