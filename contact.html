Frequently Asked Questions

What is your shipping policy?

+

We offer standard shipping on all orders over GHC 40 within Ghana. International shipping rates vary by location. Standard shipping typically takes 2-4 business days, while express shipping takes 1-2 business days.

What is your return policy?

+

We accept returns within Three days of purchase for unused and unopened products. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, please contact our customer service team to initiate a return.

Are your products suitable for sensitive skin?

+

Many of our products are formulated with sensitive skin in mind. We recommend checking the specific product descriptions or contacting us directly if you have specific skin concerns or allergies.

Do you offer samples?

+

Yes! We offer sample sizes of most products. You can purchase sample sets on our website or receive free samples with orders over GHC500.
