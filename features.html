Natural Ingredients

At Bernisbeauty, we believe in the power of nature. Our products are formulated with carefully selected natural ingredients that nourish and protect your skin.

We source our ingredients from sustainable farms and suppliers who share our commitment to ethical practices and environmental stewardship.

  • Organic botanical extracts
  • Cold-pressed essential oils
  • Plant-derived moisturizers
  • No synthetic fragrances or colors
  • Free from parabens and sulfates

Cruelty-Free Promise

We are proud to be 100% cruelty-free. We never test our products on animals and work only with suppliers who uphold the same ethical standards.

Our commitment to cruelty-free practices extends throughout our entire supply chain, from raw materials to finished products.

  • Certified cruelty-free by Leaping Bunny
  • Alternative testing methods
  • Ethical supplier verification
  • Vegan-friendly formulations
  • Supporting animal welfare initiatives

Sustainable Packaging

Our commitment to the planet extends to our packaging. We've designed our product containers and shipping materials to minimize environmental impact.

We're constantly innovating to reduce waste and increase the recyclability and biodegradability of our packaging.

  • Recycled and recyclable materials
  • Biodegradable shipping materials
  • Refillable product options
  • Minimal plastic usage
  • Carbon-neutral shipping

Dermatologist Tested

All our products undergo rigorous dermatological testing to ensure they're gentle and effective for all skin types, including sensitive skin.

We work with leading dermatologists to develop formulations that deliver results without causing irritation or adverse reactions.

  • Tested for all skin types
  • Non-comedogenic formulations
  • Hypoallergenic options available
  • pH-balanced for skin health
  • Clinically proven results
